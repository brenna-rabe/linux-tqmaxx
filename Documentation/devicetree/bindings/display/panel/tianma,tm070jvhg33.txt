Tianma Micro-electronics TM070JVHG33 7.0" WXGA TFT LCD panel

Required properties:
- compatible: should be "tianma,tm070jvhg33" or "tianma,tm070jvhg33_mx8m"

second compatible is for usage with i.MX8M/i.MX8MN/i.MX8MN with DSI to LVDS
display bridge

This binding is compatible with the simple-panel binding, which is specified
in simple-panel.txt in this directory.
